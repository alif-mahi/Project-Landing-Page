The Wheel Of Time

Written by Robert Jordan and completed posthumously by Brandon Sanderson, The Wheel of Time is one of the greatest epics of fantasy and a #1 internationally bestselling series. Taking place both in our past and our future, the saga tells the story of a man destined to face the Evil One and save the world—or destroy it in the process.The Wheel of Time turns and Ages come and pass. What was, what will be, and what is, may yet fall under the Shadow.
